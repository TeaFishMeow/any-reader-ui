**例12.1.2** 计算行列式

$$
A = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} - 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 203 & 298 & 399 \\ \frac {1}{3} & \frac {1}{2} & \frac {2}{3} \end{array} \right|.
$$

**解** 为避免分数运算，首先利用性质8，以6乘第3行，并在行列式前乘上因子 $\frac{1}{6}$ ，然后再利用性质3,得

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = \frac {1}{6} \left| \begin{array}{c c c} - 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 203 & 298 & 399 \\ 2 & 3 & 4 \end{array} \right| = \frac {1}{6} \left| \begin{array}{c c c} - 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 200 + 3 & 300 - 2 & 400 - 1 \\ 2 & 3 & 4 \end{array} \right| \\ = \frac {1}{6} \left\{\left| \begin{array}{c c c} - 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 200 & 300 & 400 \\ 2 & 3 & 4 \end{array} \right| + \left| \begin{array}{c c c} - 1 & 3 & 5 \\ 3 & - 2 & - 1 \\ 2 & 3 & 4 \end{array} \right| \right\}, \\ \end{array}
$$

由性质10,右端第一个行列式等于零，按第一行展开第二个行列式，得

$$
A = \frac {1}{6} (- 1 \times (- 5) - 3 \times 14 + 5 \times 13) = \frac {14}{3}.
$$

**例12.1.3** 证明：

$$
\left| \begin{array}{c c c} a _ {1} + b _ {1} & b _ {1} + c _ {1} & c _ {1} + a _ {1} \\ a _ {2} + b _ {2} & b _ {2} + c _ {2} & c _ {2} + a _ {2} \\ a _ {3} + b _ {3} & b _ {3} + c _ {3} & c _ {3} + a _ {3} \end{array} \right| = 2 \left| \begin{array}{c c c} a _ {1} & b _ {1} & c _ {1} \\ a _ {2} & b _ {2} & c _ {2} \\ a _ {3} & b _ {3} & c _ {3} \end{array} \right|.
$$

**证**

设

$$
\alpha = \left( \begin{array}{c} a _ {1} \\ a _ {2} \\ a _ {3} \end{array} \right), \quad \beta = \left( \begin{array}{c} b _ {1} \\ b _ {2} \\ b _ {3} \end{array} \right), \quad \gamma = \left( \begin{array}{c} c _ {1} \\ c _ {2} \\ c _ {3} \end{array} \right).
$$

则左边行列式可写为

$$
\left| \alpha + \beta, \beta + \gamma, \gamma + \alpha \right|.
$$

按性质3对各列分别展开，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \alpha + \beta, \beta + \gamma, \gamma + \alpha \right| \\ = \left| \alpha, \beta, \gamma \right| + \left| \alpha, \beta, \alpha \right| + \left| \alpha, \gamma, \gamma \right| + \left| \alpha, \gamma, \alpha \right| \\ \quad + \left| \beta, \beta, \gamma \right| + \left| \beta, \beta, \alpha \right| + \left| \beta, \gamma, \gamma \right| + \left| \beta, \gamma, \alpha \right|. \\ \end{array}
$$

其中含有两列相同的行列式都等于零（性质6），故

$$
\left| \alpha + \beta, \beta + \gamma, \gamma + \alpha \right| = \left| \alpha, \beta, \gamma \right| + \left| \beta, \gamma, \alpha \right|.
$$

而

$$
\left| \beta, \gamma, \alpha \right| = \left| \alpha, \beta, \gamma \right|
$$

这是因为把三列循环移位两次相当于作两次列交换，行列式值不变（性质5）．因此

$$
\left| \alpha + \beta, \beta + \gamma, \gamma + \alpha \right| = 2 \left| \alpha, \beta, \gamma \right|,
$$

即

$$
\left| \begin{array}{c c c} a _ {1} + b _ {1} & b _ {1} + c _ {1} & c _ {1} + a _ {1} \\ a _ {2} + b _ {2} & b _ {2} + c _ {2} & c _ {2} + a _ {2} \\ a _ {3} + b _ {3} & b _ {3} + c _ {3} & c _ {3} + a _ {3} \end{array} \right| = 2 \left| \begin{array}{c c c} a _ {1} & b _ {1} & c _ {1} \\ a _ {2} & b _ {2} & c _ {2} \\ a _ {3} & b _ {3} & c _ {3} \end{array} \right|.
$$

$R_{i} + kR_{j}$ 表示：将第 $j$ 行的 $k$ 倍加到第 $i$ 行，亦即第 $i$ 行加上第 $j$ 行的 $k$ 倍；

$R_{i}\leftrightarrow R_{j}$ 表示：将第 $i$ 行与第 $j$ 行交换；

$kR_{i}$ 表示：将第 $i$ 行乘以 $k$

如果运算是对列施行的，只要在这些记号中将 $R$ 改写为 $C$ ，即相应的记号为 $C_{i}+$

$$
k C _ {j}, C _ {i} \leftrightarrow C _ {j}, k C _ {i}.
$$

(3) 有时, 行列式的某一行 (列) 有多个元素是零, 按该行 (列) 展开, 就可以归结为计算少数几个低一阶的行列式, 当然计算起来就比较方便. 由此可见, 即使行列式没有较多元素为零的行 (列), 若可以利用行列式的性质将某行 (列) 化为只有一、两个非零元素, 则按该行 (列) 展开也就易于计算. 并且对于低一级的行列式仍可如法炮制, 直到最后变成计算一、两个二阶行列式. 注意应选择含有 1 或 -1 的行 (列) 进行变化, 以避免分数运算.

**例12.1.7** 计算行列式

$$
D = \left| \begin{array}{r r r r} 5 & 2 & - 6 & - 3 \\ - 4 & 7 & - 2 & 4 \\ - 2 & 3 & 4 & 1 \\ 7 & - 8 & -10 & - 5 \end{array} \right|.

$$

$$

**例12.1.8** 证明 $n$ 阶范德蒙德(Vandermonde)行列式 $(n\geqslant 2)$

$$
D _ {n} = \left| \begin{array}{c c c c c} 1 & 1 & 1 & \dots & 1 \\ a _ {1} & a _ {2} & a _ {3} & \dots & a _ {n} \\ a _ {1} ^ {2} & a _ {2} ^ {2} & a _ {3} ^ {2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {2} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {1} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {2} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 2} \\ a _ {1} ^ {n - 1} & a _ {2} ^ {n - 1} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 1} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 1} \end{array} \right| = \prod_ {1 \leqslant j <   i \leqslant n} (a _ {i} - a _ {j}).
$$

这里

$$
\prod_ {1 \leqslant j <   i \leqslant n} (a _ {i} - a _ {j}) = (a _ {2} - a _ {1}) (a _ {3} - a _ {1}) \dots (a _ {n - 1} - a _ {1}) (a _ {n} - a _ {1})
$$

$$
\cdot (a _ {3} - a _ {2}) \dots (a _ {n - 1} - a _ {2}) (a _ {n} - a _ {2})
$$

其中

$$
\cdot \left(a _ {n} - a _ {n - 1}\right).
$$

**证** 对阶数 $n$ 应用数学归纳法. 对于 $n = 2$ ,

$$
D _ {2} = \left| \begin{array}{c c} 1 & 1 \\ a _ {1} & a _ {2} \end{array} \right| = a _ {2} - a _ {1},
$$

可见结论成立.

假设结论对 $(n - 1)$ 成立，即

$$
D _ {n - 1} = \left| \begin{array}{c c c c} 1 & 1 & \dots & 1 \\ a _ {2} & a _ {3} & \dots & a _ {n} \\ a _ {2} ^ {2} & a _ {3} ^ {2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {2} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {2} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 2} \end{array} \right| = \prod_ {2 \leqslant j <   i \leqslant n} (a _ {i} - a _ {j}),
$$

现在考察 $n$ 阶的情况. 将 $(n - 1)$ 行乘以 $(-a_{1})$ 加到第 $n$ 行，再将 $(n - 2)$ 行乘以

$(-a_{1})$ 加到第 $(n - 1)$ 行，依此类推，直到将第一行乘以 $(-a_{1})$ 加到第二行，得到

$$
\begin{array}{l} D _ {n} = \left| \begin{array}{c c c c c} 1 & 1 & 1 & \dots & 1 \\ 0 & a _ {2} - a _ {1} & a _ {3} - a _ {1} & \dots & a _ {n} - a _ {1} \\ 0 & a _ {2} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {2} & a _ {3} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {3} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ 0 & a _ {2} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {2} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {3} ^ {n - 2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {n} ^ {n - 2} \end{array} \right| \\ = \left| \begin{array}{c c c c} a _ {2} - a _ {1} & a _ {3} - a _ {1} & \dots & a _ {n} - a _ {1} \\ a _ {2} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {2} & a _ {3} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {3} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {2} - a _ {1} a _ {n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {2} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {2} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {3} ^ {n - 2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 1} - a _ {1} a _ {n} ^ {n - 2} \end{array} \right| \\ = (a _ {2} - a _ {1}) (a _ {3} - a _ {1}) \dots (a _ {n} - a _ {1}) \left| \begin{array}{c c c c} 1 & 1 & \dots & 1 \\ a _ {2} & a _ {3} & \dots & a _ {n} \\ a _ {2} ^ {2} & a _ {3} ^ {2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {2} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {2} ^ {n - 2} & a _ {3} ^ {n - 2} & \dots & a _ {n} ^ {n - 2} \end{array} \right|, \\ \end{array}
$$

按归纳假设，右端的行列式等于 $\prod_{2\leqslant j <   i\leqslant n}(a_i - a_j)$ ，于是

$$
D _ {n} = (a _ {2} - a _ {1}) (a _ {3} - a _ {1}) \dots (a _ {n} - a _ {1}) \prod_ {2 \leqslant j <   i \leqslant n} (a _ {i} - a _ {j}) = \prod_ {1 \leqslant j <   i \leqslant n} (a _ {i} - a _ {j}).
$$

即结论对于 $n$ 也成立.
