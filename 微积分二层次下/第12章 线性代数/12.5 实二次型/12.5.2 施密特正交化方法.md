定理12.5.1 正交向量组是线性无关的

证设 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{s}$ 是一正交向量组，并且

$$
k _ {1} \alpha_ {1} + k _ {2} \alpha_ {2} + \dots + k _ {s} \alpha_ {s} = \theta .
$$

则

$$
\left(k _ {1} \alpha_ {1} + k _ {2} \alpha_ {2} + \dots + k _ {s} \alpha_ {s}, \alpha_ {i}\right) = 0 (i = 1, 2, \dots , s),
$$

此即

$$
k _ {1} (\alpha_ {1}, \alpha_ {i}) + k _ {2} (\alpha_ {2}, \alpha_ {i}) + \dots + k _ {s} (\alpha_ {s}, \alpha_ {i}) = 0.
$$

由于 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{s}$ 两两正交，且均非零向量，故上式左端除 $(\alpha_{i},\alpha_{i})\neq 0$ 外，其余诸内积皆为零，故上式成为 $k_{i}(\pmb{\alpha}_{i},\pmb{\alpha}_{i}) = 0,$ 由此又知 $k_{i}$ $(i = 1,2,\dots ,s)$ 必须为零，故 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{s}$ 线性无关. □

定理12.5.1的逆定理是不对的，即：线性无关的向量组未必是正交向量组。但从一组线性无关的向量组 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{s}$ 出发，利用它们的线性组合，总可以构造出一组向量正交组 $\gamma_1,\gamma_2,\dots ,\gamma_s$

这可以通过下面的施密特正交化方法来完成. 在正交化以后, 再由 $\gamma_1, \gamma_2, \dots, \gamma_s$ 产生一组长度为 1 的向量 $\beta_1, \beta_2, \dots, \beta_s$ , 此即单位化. 如此, 从给定的线性无关向量组 $\alpha_1, \alpha_2, \dots, \alpha_s$ , 出发, 总可以通过它们的线性组合, 构造一组规范正交向量组 $\beta_1, \beta_2, \dots, \beta_s$ , 其详细做法如下:

(1) 取 $\gamma_{1} = \alpha_{1}$ .   
(2) 取 $\gamma_{2} = \alpha_{2} - \frac{(\alpha_{2},\gamma_{1})}{(\gamma_{1},\gamma_{1})}\gamma_{1}$ , 则 $\gamma_{2}$ 是 $\alpha_{2},\alpha_{1}$ 的线性组合, 且

$$
(\gamma_ {2}, \gamma_ {1}) = (\alpha_ {2}, \gamma_ {1}) - \frac {(\alpha_ {2} , \gamma_ {1})}{(\gamma_ {1} , \gamma_ {1})} (\gamma_ {1}, \gamma_ {1}) = 0.
$$

即 $\gamma_{2}$ 与 $\gamma_{1}$ 正交

(3) 取 $\gamma_{3} = \alpha_{3} - \frac{(\alpha_{3},\gamma_{2})}{(\gamma_{2},\gamma_{2})}\gamma_{2} - \frac{(\alpha_{3},\gamma_{1})}{(\gamma_{1},\gamma_{1})}\gamma_{1}$ , 则 $\gamma_{3}$ 是 $\alpha_{3},\alpha_{2},\alpha_{1}$ 的线性组合, 且

$$
\begin{array}{l} (\gamma_ {3}, \gamma_ {2}) = (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {2}) - \frac {(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {2})}{(\gamma_ {2} , \gamma_ {2})} (\gamma_ {2}, \gamma_ {2}) - \frac {(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {1})}{(\gamma_ {1} , \gamma_ {1})} (\gamma_ {2}, \gamma_ {1}) \\ = (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {2}) - (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {2}) - 0 = 0, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} (\gamma_ {3}, \gamma_ {1}) = (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {1}) - \frac {(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {2})}{(\gamma_ {2} , \gamma_ {2})} (\gamma_ {2}, \gamma_ {1}) - \frac {(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {1})}{(\gamma_ {1} , \gamma_ {1})} (\gamma_ {1}, \gamma_ {1}) \\ = (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {1}) - 0 - (\alpha_ {3}, \gamma_ {1}) = 0, \\ \end{array}
$$

即 $\gamma_{3}$ 与 $\gamma_{2},\gamma_{1}$ 都正交

依次类推，直到

取 $\gamma_{s} = \alpha_{s} - \frac{(\alpha_{s},\gamma_{s - 1})}{(\gamma_{s - 1},\gamma_{s - 1})}\gamma_{s - 1} - \dots -\frac{(\alpha_{s},\gamma_{1})}{(\gamma_{1},\gamma_{1})}\gamma_{1}$ 则 $\gamma_{s}$ 是 $\alpha_{s},\alpha_{s - 1},\dots ,\alpha_{1}$ 的线性组合，且与 $\gamma_1,\gamma_2,\dots ,\gamma_{s - 1}$ 都正交

于是，得到了两两正交的向量组 $\gamma_{1},\gamma_{2},\dots ,\gamma_{s}$ ，这就完成了正交化．为了单位化，只需令

$$
\beta_ {1} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {1} |} \gamma_ {1}, \quad \beta_ {2} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {2} |} \gamma_ {2}, \quad \dots , \quad \beta_ {s} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {s} |} \gamma_ {s},
$$

这样，就得到了以 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{s}$ 的线性组合所构成的规范正交组 $\beta_{1},\beta_{2},\dots ,\beta_{s}$ 这过程称为规范正交化过程.

例12.5.2 将线性无关向量组

$$
\alpha_ {1} = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right), \quad \alpha_ {2} = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right), \quad \alpha_ {3} = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right),
$$

规范正交化.

解 首先正交化，取

$$
\gamma_ {1} = \alpha_ {1} = \left( \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right)
$$

$$
\gamma_ {2} = \alpha_ {2} - \frac {\left(\alpha_ {2} , \gamma_ {1}\right)}{\left(\gamma_ {1} , \gamma_ {1}\right)} \gamma_ {1} = \left( \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 0 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) - \frac {1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{l} 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{l} 1 / 2 \\ - 1 / 2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right),
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \gamma_ {3} = \alpha_ {3} - \frac {\left(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {2}\right)}{\left(\gamma_ {2} , \gamma_ {2}\right)} \gamma_ {2} - \frac {\left(\alpha_ {3} , \gamma_ {1}\right)}{\left(\gamma_ {1} , \gamma_ {1}\right)} \gamma_ {1} = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right) - \frac {1}{3} \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 / 2 \\ - 1 / 2 \\ 1 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) - \frac {1}{2} \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 \\ 1 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right) \\ = \left( \begin{array}{c} 1 / 3 \\ - 1 / 3 \\ - 1 / 3 \\ - 1 \end{array} \right). \\ \end{array}
$$

再单位化，即得所求的规范正交组：

$$
\begin{array}{l} \beta_ {1} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {1} |} \gamma_ {1} = \left( \begin{array}{c} \sqrt {2} / 2 \\ \sqrt {2} / 2 \\ 0 \\ 0 \end{array} \right), \beta_ {2} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {2} |} \gamma_ {2} = \left( \begin{array}{c} \sqrt {6} / 6 \\ - \sqrt {6} / 6 \\ \sqrt {6} / 3 \\ 0 \end{array} \right), \\ \beta_ {3} = \frac {1}{| \gamma_ {3} |} \gamma_ {3} = \left( \begin{array}{c} \sqrt {3} / 6 \\ - \sqrt {3} / 6 \\ - \sqrt {3} / 6 \\ - \sqrt {3} / 2 \end{array} \right) \\ \end{array}
$$

如果再增加一个向量 $\alpha_{4}$ , 使 $\alpha_{1}, \alpha_{2}, \alpha_{3}, \alpha_{4}$ 是线性无关组, 则按照上述方法继续求出与 $\beta_{1}, \beta_{2}, \beta_{3}$ 都正交的单位向量 $\beta_{4}$ , 则以 $\beta_{1}, \beta_{2}, \beta_{3}, \beta_{4}$ 为列向量 (或以 $\beta_{1}^{\mathrm{T}}, \beta_{2}^{\mathrm{T}}, \beta_{3}^{\mathrm{T}}, \beta_{4}^{\mathrm{T}}$ 为行向量) 的矩阵为正交矩阵.
