定义12.5.1(向量的内积）设 $\alpha, \beta$ 是两个 $n$ 维实向量，分别以 $a_1, a_2, \dots, a_n$ 和 $b_1, b_2, \dots, b_n$ 为分量，则称 $a_1b_1 + a_2b_2 + \dots + a_nb_n$ 为 $\alpha, \beta$ 的内积，记为 $(\alpha, \beta)$ 。由定义立刻可知：

$$
\begin{array}{l} (\alpha , \beta) = (\beta , \alpha), \\ (\alpha_ {1} + \alpha_ {2}, \beta) = (\alpha_ {1}, \beta) + (\alpha_ {2}, \beta), \\ \end{array}
$$

$(k\alpha ,\beta) = k(\alpha ,\beta),k$ 为实数，

$(\alpha, \alpha) \geqslant 0$ ，当且仅当 $\alpha = \theta$ 时， $(\alpha, \alpha) = 0$

把向量看成一行或一列的矩阵，则内积 $(\alpha, \beta)$ 可以用矩阵的乘积来表示：若 $\alpha, \beta$ 为列向量，

$$
\alpha = \left( \begin{array}{c} a _ {1} \\ a _ {2} \\ \vdots \\ a _ {n} \end{array} \right), \quad \beta = \left( \begin{array}{c} b _ {1} \\ b _ {2} \\ \vdots \\ b _ {n} \end{array} \right),
$$

则

$$
(\boldsymbol {\alpha}, \boldsymbol {\beta}) = (a _ {1} a _ {2} \dots a _ {n}) \left( \begin{array}{c} b _ {1} \\ b _ {2} \\ \vdots \\ b _ {n} \end{array} \right) = \boldsymbol {\alpha} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \boldsymbol {\beta};
$$

若 $\alpha, \beta$ 为行向量，

$$
\alpha = \left(a _ {1}, a _ {2}, \dots , a _ {n}\right), \quad \beta = \left(b _ {1}, b _ {2}, \dots , b _ {n}\right),
$$

则

$$
(\boldsymbol {\alpha}, \boldsymbol {\beta}) = (a _ {1} a _ {2} \dots a _ {n}) \left( \begin{array}{c} b _ {1} \\ b _ {2} \\ \vdots \\ b _ {n} \end{array} \right) = \boldsymbol {\alpha} \boldsymbol {\beta} ^ {\mathrm {T}}.
$$

如果 $(\alpha, \beta) = 0$ ，则说 $\alpha, \beta$ 正交。由于

$$
(\theta , \alpha) = 0 \cdot a _ {1} + 0 \cdot a _ {2} + \dots + 0 \cdot a _ {n} = 0,
$$

可知零向量与任何向量正交

定义12.5.2（向量的长度） $\sqrt{(\alpha,\alpha)}$ 称为向量 $\alpha$ 的长度，记为 $|\alpha|$ 或 $|\alpha|$ ：

$$
| \alpha | = \sqrt {a _ {1} ^ {2} + a _ {2} ^ {2} + \cdots + a _ {n} ^ {2}}.
$$

定义12.5.2是平面上的向量和空间中的向量的长度的直接推广.当且仅当 $\alpha =$ $\pmb{\theta}$ 时 $|\alpha | = 0$

长度为1的向量称为单位向量. 显然，对任何非零向量 $\alpha$ ， $\frac{1}{|\alpha|}\alpha$ 是单位向量

例12.5.1 若 $\alpha = (1,k, - 2,5)$ ，试确定 $k$ 使 $|\alpha | = \sqrt{39}$ ，然后再求 $\frac{1}{|\alpha|}\alpha .$

解 由 $|\alpha| = \sqrt{39}$ 得 $1^2 + k^2 + (-2)^2 + 5^2 = 39$ , 即 $k^2 = 9$ , 故 $k = \pm 3$ . 当 $k = 3$ 时,

$$
\frac {1}{| \alpha |} \alpha = \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {3}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {- 2}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {5}{\sqrt {39}}\right),
$$

当 $k = -3$ 时

$$
\frac {1}{| \alpha |} \alpha = \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {- 3}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {- 2}{\sqrt {39}}, \frac {5}{\sqrt {39}}\right).
$$

定义12.5.3（正交矩阵）若矩阵 $\mathbf{A}$ 使得 $A^{\mathrm{T}}A = E$ ，则称 $\mathbf{A}$ 为正交矩阵

$$
\left( \begin{array}{l l} \cos \theta & - \sin \theta \\ \sin \theta & \cos \theta \end{array} \right), \left( \begin{array}{l l l} 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & - 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & - 1 \end{array} \right), \left( \begin{array}{r r r} \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} & \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} & \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \\ 0 & - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} & \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \\ - \frac {2}{\sqrt {6}} & \frac {1}{\sqrt {6}} & \frac {1}{\sqrt {6}} \end{array} \right)
$$

都是正交矩阵.

设

$$
\boldsymbol {A} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c c} a _ {1 1} & a _ {1 2} & \dots & a _ {1 n} \\ a _ {2 1} & a _ {2 2} & \dots & a _ {2 n} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {n 1} & a _ {n 2} & \dots & a _ {n n} \end{array} \right)
$$

为正交矩阵，记其列向量为 $\alpha_{1},\alpha_{2},\dots ,\alpha_{n}$ ，则

$$
\boldsymbol {A} = \left(\alpha_ {1} \alpha_ {2} \dots \alpha_ {n}\right), \quad \boldsymbol {A} ^ {\mathrm {T}} = \left( \begin{array}{c c c c} a _ {1 1} & a _ {2 1} & \dots & a _ {n 1} \\ a _ {1 2} & a _ {2 2} & \dots & a _ {n 2} \\ \vdots & \vdots & & \vdots \\ a _ {1 n} & a _ {2 n} & \dots & a _ {n n} \end{array} \right) = \left( \begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {1} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \\ \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {2} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \\ \vdots \\ \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {n} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \end{array} \right),
$$

由 $A^{\mathrm{T}}A = E$ 得

$$
\left( \begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {1} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \\ \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {2} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \\ \vdots \\ \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {n} ^ {\mathrm {T}} \end{array} \right) (\boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {1} \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {2} \dots \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {n}) = \left( \begin{array}{c c c c} 1 & & & O \\ & 1 & & \\ & & \ddots & \\ O & & & 1 \end{array} \right),
$$

可见

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} (\boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {i}) = 1, & (i \neq j, i, j = 1, 2, \dots , n) \\ (\boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {\alpha} _ {j}) = 0. \end{array} \right.
$$

即：正交矩阵的全部列向量都是单位向量，且两两彼此正交

另一方面，若 $A^{\mathrm{T}}A = E$ ，则 $A^{-1} = A^{\mathrm{T}}$ ，而由 $AA^{-1} = E$ 又得 $AA^{\mathrm{T}} = E.$ 于是，若记 $\pmb{A}$ 的行向量为 $\beta_{1},\beta_{2},\dots ,\beta_{n}$ ，则由 $AA^{\mathrm{T}} = E$ 又得

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l l} (\boldsymbol {\beta} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {\beta} _ {i}) = 1, & (i \neq j, i, j = 1, 2, \dots , n) \\ (\boldsymbol {\beta} _ {i}, \boldsymbol {\beta} _ {j}) = 0. \end{array} \right.
$$

即：正交矩阵的全部行向量也都是单位向量，且也两两彼此正交

两两正交的不含零向量的向量组称为正交向量组；由单位向量所组成的正交向量组称为单位正交向量组或规范正交组

于是：正交矩阵的行向量组和列向量组都是规范正交组。反之，列向量组或行向量组为规范正交组的矩阵为正交矩阵。
