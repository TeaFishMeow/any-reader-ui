利用多元复合函数的求导方法，从一元函数的泰勒公式出发，我们来建立二元函数的泰勒公式，在下一节，将看到这一公式在讨论二元函数的极值时的初步应用.

我们知道，如果函数 $F(t)$ 在 $t_0$ 的邻域内有直到 $n + 1$ 阶的各阶导数，则当 $|\Delta t|$ 充分小时成立泰勒公式（定理3.3.1）

$$
\begin{array}{l} F (t _ {0} + \Delta t) = F (t _ {0}) + \frac {F ^ {\prime} (t _ {0})}{1 !} \Delta t + \frac {F ^ {\prime \prime} (t _ {0})}{2 !} \Delta t ^ {2} + \dots + \frac {F ^ {(n)} (t _ {0})}{n !} \Delta t ^ {n} \tag {9.13} \\ + \frac {F ^ {(n + 1)} \left(t _ {0} + \theta \Delta t\right)}{(n + 1) !} \Delta t ^ {n + 1} \quad (0 <   \theta <   1). \\ \end{array}
$$

现在设函数 $f(x,y)$ 在点 $(x_0,y_0)$ 的邻域内直到 $n + 1$ 阶的一切偏导数都是连续的，取 $|\Delta x|$ 及 $|\Delta y|$ 充分小，使连接点 $(x_0,y_0)$ 及点 $(x_0 + \Delta x,y_0 + \Delta y)$ 的线段位于所述邻域内，令

$$
x = x _ {0} + t \Delta x, \quad y = y _ {0} + t \Delta y, \quad 0 \leqslant t \leqslant 1,
$$

则 $f(x,y)$ 通过中间变量 $x,y$ 而成为 $t$ 的函数：

$$
F (t) = \dot {f} \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right),
$$

并且

$$
F (1) = f \left(x _ {0} + \Delta x, y _ {0} + \Delta y\right), \quad F (0) = f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right). \tag {9.14}
$$

按链锁法则，

$$
\begin{array}{l} F ^ {\prime} (t) = \frac {\partial f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right)}{\partial x} \Delta x + \frac {\partial f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right)}{\partial y} \Delta y \\ = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right), \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} F ^ {\prime \prime} (t) = \frac {\mathrm {d}}{\mathrm {d t}} \left[ \frac {\partial f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right)}{\partial x} \Delta x + \frac {\partial f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right)}{\partial y} \Delta y \right] \\ = \frac {\partial^ {2} f (x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y)}{\partial x ^ {2}} \Delta x ^ {2} + 2 \frac {\partial^ {2} f (x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y)}{\partial x \partial y} \Delta x \Delta y \\ + \frac {\partial^ {2} f (x _ {0} + t \Delta x , y _ {0} + t \Delta y)}{\partial y ^ {2}} \Delta y ^ {2} \\ = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right), \\ \end{array}
$$

···

一般地

$$
F ^ {(n)} (t) = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n} f \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta t, y _ {0} + t \Delta y\right). \tag {9.15}
$$

(9.15) 中所出现的 $\Delta x\frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y\frac{\partial}{\partial y}, \left(\Delta x\frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right)^2, \dots, \left(\Delta x\frac{\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\right)^n$ 是为了书写方便又易于记忆而引用的运算符号，按乘法将括弧形式地展开，并将括弧外面的 $f(x_0 + t\Delta x, y_0 + t\Delta y)$ 分配到括弧中的每一项，并一律记在 $\partial^k$ 的后面，则所有这些记号就恢复了偏导数的意义而得到 $F'(t), F''(t), \dots, F^{(n)}(t)$ 的真正表达式。

由 (9.15) 得

$$
\left. \begin{array}{l} F ^ {\prime} (0) = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right), \\ F ^ {\prime \prime} (0) = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right), \\ \dots \dots \\ F ^ {(n)} (0) = \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n} f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right). \end{array} \right\} \tag {9.16}
$$

在 (9.13) 中取 $t_0 = 0, \Delta t = 1,$ 得

$$
F (1) = F (0) + F ^ {\prime} (0) + \frac {1}{2 !} F ^ {\prime \prime} (0) + \dots + \frac {1}{n !} F ^ {(n)} (0) + \frac {1}{(n + 1) !} F ^ {(n + 1)} (\theta).
$$

将(9.14)、(9.16)以及

$$
F ^ {(n + 1)} (\theta) = \frac {1}{(n + 1) !} \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n + 1} f (x _ {0} + \theta \Delta x, y _ {0} + \theta \Delta y)
$$

代入上式得

$$
\begin{array}{l} f (x _ {0} + \Delta x, y _ {0} + \Delta y) \\ = f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right) + \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right) + \frac {1}{2 !} \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right) \\ + \dots + \frac {1}{n !} \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n} f (x _ {0}, y _ {0}) \\ + \frac {1}{(n + 1) !} \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n + 1} f \left(x _ {0} + \theta \Delta x, y _ {0} + \theta \Delta y\right) \quad (0 <   \theta <   1). \tag {9.17} \\ \end{array}
$$

公式 (9.17) 称为带有拉格朗日余项的泰勒公式, 而

$$
R _ {n} = \frac {1}{(n + 1) !} \left(\Delta x \frac {\partial}{\partial x} + \Delta y \frac {\partial}{\partial y}\right) ^ {n + 1} f (x _ {0} + \theta \Delta x, y _ {0} + \theta \Delta y)
$$

称为拉格朗日余项

在 (9.17) 中将 $f(x_0, y_0)$ 移至等式左端并抛弃余项 $R_n$ 就得到以 $\Delta x, \Delta y$ 的 $n$ 次多项式近似地表示函数增量 $f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0 + \Delta y) - f(x_0, y_0)$ 的公式，可以证明，由此产生的误差是 $\rho^n = (\sqrt{\Delta x^2 + \Delta y^2})^n$ 的高阶无穷小： $R_n = o(\rho^n)$ . 在 (9.17) 中取 $n = 0$ ，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} f \left(x _ {0} + \Delta x, y _ {0} + \Delta y\right) = f \left(x _ {0}, y _ {0}\right) + f _ {x} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + \theta \Delta x, y _ {0} + \theta \Delta y\right) \Delta x \tag {9.18} \\ + f _ {y} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + \theta \Delta x, y _ {0} + \theta \Delta y\right) \Delta y \quad (0 <   \theta <   1), \\ \end{array}
$$

称之为二元函数的拉格朗日中值定理. 由此可见, 若在某区域内偏导数 $f_{x}^{\prime}(x,y)$ 及 $f_{y}^{\prime}(x,y)$ 恒等于零, 则在这个区域内函数 $f(x,y)$ 恒等于常数: $f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0 + \Delta y) \equiv f(x_0, y_0)$ , 亦即 $f(x,y) \equiv f(x_0, y_0)$ .
