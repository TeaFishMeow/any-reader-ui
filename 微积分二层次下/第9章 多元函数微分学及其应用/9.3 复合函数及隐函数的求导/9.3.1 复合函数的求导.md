设 $z = f(u,v)$ 是变量 $u,v$ 的函数，而变量 $u,v$ 又是另一些变量（一个或多个）例如 $x,y$ 的函数 $u = u(x,y),v = v(x,y)$ ，于是 $\textit{\textbf{z}}$ 成为复合函数 $z = f(u(x,y),v(x,y))$ 当函数 $f,u,v$ 的初等函数表达式具体给出时，为了求复合函数对 $x,y$ 的偏导数，只需用到一元函数的链锁公式就行了（在9.2节，我们已经这样做了).可是，当这些的具体表达式没有给出，而仅用抽象的函数记号 $f,\varphi ,\psi$ 表示为 $z = f(u,v),u =$ $\varphi (x,y),v = \psi (x,y)$ 时，或是 $f,\varphi ,\psi$ 的具体表示式虽已给出，但为了避免将 $\varphi ,\psi$ 代入 $f$ 所得到的复杂的表示式时，就需要通过偏导数 $f_{u}^{\prime},f_{v}^{\prime},u_{x}^{\prime},u_{y}^{\prime},v_{x}^{\prime},v_{y}^{\prime}$ 直接表示复合函数的偏导数 $z_x^\prime ,z_y^\prime$ 了，现在，我们着手讨论这一问题

设函数 $z = f(u,v)$ 以及 $u = \varphi (x,y),v = \psi (x,y)$ 各自对自己的所有变元有一阶偏导数，并且这些偏导数都是连续的．如果 $x$ 获得增量 $\Delta x,$ 则 $u$ 和 $v$ 获得相应的增量 $\Delta u$ 和 $\Delta v,$ 从而 $z$ 有增量 $\Delta z.$ 由于 $z_u^{\prime}$ 和 $z_v^\prime$ 是连续的，类似于(9.3）可得

$$
\Delta z = f _ {u} ^ {\prime} \Delta u + f _ {v} ^ {\prime} \Delta v + \alpha \Delta u + \beta \Delta v,
$$

并且，当 $\Delta u\to 0,\Delta v\to 0$ 时， $\alpha \rightarrow 0,\beta \rightarrow 0,$ 以 $\Delta x$ 除上式两端得

$$
\frac {\Delta z}{\Delta x} = f _ {u} ^ {\prime} \frac {\Delta u}{\Delta x} + f _ {v} ^ {\prime} \frac {\Delta v}{\Delta x} + \alpha \frac {\Delta u}{\Delta x} + \beta \frac {\Delta v}{\Delta x}.
$$

由于 $\varphi (x,y)$ ， $\psi (x,y)$ 的偏导数连续，因而可微，从而 $u = \varphi (x,y)$ ， $v = \psi (x,y)$ 也是连续的．于上式令 $\Delta x\to 0$ ，于是 $\Delta u\to 0$ ， $\Delta v\to 0$ （从而 $\alpha \rightarrow 0$ ， $\beta \rightarrow 0$ )，并且 $\frac{\Delta u}{\Delta x}\rightarrow u_x^{\prime},\frac{\Delta v}{\Delta x}\rightarrow v_{x^{\prime}}$ ，而在上式两端取极限即得

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial x}, \tag {9.4}
$$

同理可证

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial y}. \tag {9.5}
$$

这就是我们所需要的公式，它们通过 $f, u, v$ 的偏导数表达了复合函数 $z$ 对直接变量 $x, y$ 的导数。由这些公式可见，在 $f, u, v$ 的一切一阶偏导数连续的假定下，复合函数的偏导数也是连续的。

和一元函数的情形一样，公式(9.4)、(9.5)称为多元复合函数求导的链锁公式.多元函数的链锁公式呈现为多种多样的形式，读者需要对这一公式所表示的法则有深刻的理解，以做到举一反三，触类旁通．例如，当函数 $z = f(u,v)$ 或函数 $z = g(u,v,w)$ 的变元 $u,v,w$ 都是一个变元 $t$ 的函数时，依次有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} t} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{\mathrm {d} t} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\mathrm {d} v}{\mathrm {d} t}, \\ \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} t} = \frac {\partial g}{\partial u} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{\mathrm {d} t} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial v} \frac {\mathrm {d} v}{\mathrm {d} t} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial w} \frac {\mathrm {d} w}{\mathrm {d} t}. \\ \end{array}
$$

$\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}t}$ 称为复合函数 $z(t)$ 的全导数. 注意，在所写出的两个公式里，既有一元函数的导数，也有多元函数的偏导数，切勿混淆

在 $z = f(u,v)$ 和 $z = g(u,v,w)$ 中，如果变量 $\mathcal{V}$ 和 $\textit{\textbf{w}}$ 都是 $\pmb{u}$ 的函数，则 $\textit{\textbf{z}}$ 成为 $\pmb{u}$ 的复合函数，此时，全导数依次成为

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} u} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\mathrm {d} v}{\mathrm {d} u}, \\ \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} u} = \frac {\partial g}{\partial u} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial v} \frac {\mathrm {d} v}{\mathrm {d} u} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial w} \frac {\mathrm {d} w}{\mathrm {d} u}. \\ \end{array}
$$

这里，特别需要注意左端的 $\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}u}$ 与右端的 $\frac{\partial f}{\partial u}, \frac{\partial g}{\partial u}$ 的不同。前者是复合后的函数 $z(u, v(u)), z(u, v(u), w(u))$ 对直接变量 $u$ 的导数，即全导数，而后者则是未经复合的二元函数 $f(u, v)$ 及三元函数 $g(u, v, w)$ 对变量 $u$ 的偏导数。

又如，若 $z = g(u,v,w)$ ，而 $w = w(u,v)$ ，则复合函数 $z = g(u,v,w(u,v))$ 是两个变量 $u,v$ 的函数，它对 $u,v$ 的偏导数为

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial u} = \frac {\partial g}{\partial u} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial w} \frac {\partial w}{\partial u}, \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} = \frac {\partial g}{\partial v} + \frac {\partial g}{\partial w} \frac {\partial w}{\partial v}.
$$

这里，同样需要注意记号 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}$ 与 $\frac{\partial g}{\partial u}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial v}$ 与 $\frac{\partial g}{\partial v}$ 的不同意义。 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}$ 与 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial v}$ 是复合后的二元函数 $g(u, v, w(u, v))$ 对变量 $u, v$ 的偏导数，而 $\frac{\partial g}{\partial u}, \frac{\partial g}{\partial v}$ 是未经复合的三元函数 $g(u, v, w)$ 对变量 $u, v$ 的偏导数。

例9.3.1 设 $z = \mathrm{e}^u\sin v, u = xy, v = x^2 +y^2$ ，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$ 和 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

解 按公式 (9.4) 和 (9.5),

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial x} = \mathrm {e} ^ {u} \sin v \cdot y + \mathrm {e} ^ {u} \cos v \cdot 2 x \\ = \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} \left\{y \sin \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) + 2 x \cos \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) \right\}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} = e ^ {u} \sin v \cdot x + e ^ {u} \cos v \cdot 2 y \\ = \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} \left\{x \sin \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) + 2 y \cos \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$

例9.3.2 设 $z(x,y) = f\left(x,xy,\frac{x}{y}\right)$ , 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$ .

解 令 $u = x, v = xy, w = \frac{x}{y}$ , 则 $z = f(u, v, w)$ , 由链锁公式, 注意 $u$ 不依赖于 $y$ , 得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial w} \frac {\partial w}{\partial x} \\ = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} + y \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} + \frac {1}{y} \frac {\partial f}{\partial w}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {\partial f}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial f}{\partial w} \frac {\partial w}{\partial y} \\ = x \frac {\partial f}{\partial v} - \frac {x}{y ^ {2}} \frac {\partial f}{\partial w}. \\ \end{array}
$$

为书写简便，对 $f(u,v,w)$ ，分别记 $f_{u}^{\prime},f_{v}^{\prime},f_{w}^{\prime}$ 为 $f_1^\prime ,f_2^\prime ,f_3^\prime$ ，在熟练掌握链锁公式的基础上，本题可以不引进中间变量 $u,v,w,$ 而直接写出结果：

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = f _ {1} ^ {\prime} + y f _ {2} ^ {\prime} + \frac {1}{y} f _ {3} ^ {\prime}, \\ \frac {\partial z}{\partial g} = x f _ {2} ^ {\prime} - \frac {x}{y ^ {2}} f _ {3} ^ {\prime}. \\ \end{array}
$$

例9.3.3 设 $z = uv + \ln (1 + t^2)$ , 其中 $u = \mathrm{e}^t$ , $v = \cos t$ , 求全导数 $\frac{\mathrm{d}z}{\mathrm{d}t}$ .

解

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} t} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{\mathrm {d} t} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} \frac {\mathrm {d} v}{\mathrm {d} t} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial t} = v \cdot \mathrm {e} ^ {t} + u \cdot (- \sin t) + \frac {2 t}{1 + t ^ {2}} \\ = \mathrm {e} ^ {t} (\cos t - \sin t) + \frac {2 t}{1 + t ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于求高阶偏导数实际上是逐次求一阶偏导数，因此，求复合函数的高阶偏导数时链锁公式仍然是有效的.

例9.3.4 设函数 $z = x\varphi(x + y) + y\psi(x + y)$ ，其中 $\varphi$ 和 $\psi$ 有二阶连续导数。证明

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} - 2 \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x \partial y} + \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}} = 0.
$$

解

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \varphi (x + y) + x \varphi^ {\prime} (x + y) + y \psi^ {\prime} (x + y), \\ \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = x \varphi^ {\prime} (x + y) + \psi (x + y) + y \psi^ {\prime} (x + y), \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} = 2 \varphi^ {\prime} (x + y) + x \varphi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y) + y \psi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y), \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x \partial y} = \varphi^ {\prime} (x + y) + x \varphi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y) + \psi^ {\prime} (x + y) + y \psi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y), \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}} = x \varphi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y) + 2 \psi^ {\prime} (x + y) + y \psi^ {\prime \prime} (x + y), \\ \end{array}
$$

将三个二阶导数代入欲求证的等式即知是成立的.

例9.3.5 设 $z = f(x, y)$ 有二阶连续偏导数， $u = x + ay$ ， $v = x - ay$ 。试用 $z$ 对 $u, v$ 的导数表示 $a^2 \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x^2} - \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y^2}$ 。

解 设想 $z$ 以 $u, v$ 为中间变量成为 $x, y$ 的函数，按链锁公式得

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v},
$$

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v} \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} = a \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} - a \frac {\partial z}{\partial v},
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}\right) = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \\ = \frac {\partial}{\partial u} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u}\right) \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial}{\partial v} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u}\right) \frac {\partial v}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial}{\partial u} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial}{\partial v} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \frac {\partial v}{\partial x} \\ = \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial u ^ {2}} + 2 \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial u \partial v} + \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial v ^ {2}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial y}\right) = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left(a \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} - a \frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \\ = a \left\{\frac {\partial}{\partial u} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u}\right) \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial}{\partial v} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u}\right) \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial}{\partial u} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial}{\partial v} \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial v}\right) \frac {\partial v}{\partial y} \right\} \\ = a \left\{a \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial u ^ {2}} - 2 a \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial u \partial v} + a \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial v ^ {2}} \right\}, \\ \end{array}
$$

由此得到

$$
a ^ {2} \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} - \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}} = 4 a ^ {2} \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial u \partial v}.
$$
