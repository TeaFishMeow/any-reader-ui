1. 求复合函数的全导数：

1) $z = \frac{y}{x}$ , 而 $x = \mathrm{e}^t, y = 1 - \mathrm{e}^{2t}$ ;   
2) $z = \mathrm{e}^{x - 2y}$ , 而 $x = \sin t, y = t^3$ ;   
3) $z = \tan (3t - x + 2y^2)$ , 而 $x = \sqrt{t}, y = \frac{1}{t}$ .

2. 设 $z = x^{2}y - xy^{2}$ , 而 $x = u\cos v, y = u\sin v$ , 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial u}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial v}$ .   
3. 若 $z = \frac{u^2}{v}$ , 而 $u = x - 2y$ , $v = 2x + y$ , 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$ .  
4. $z = \mathrm{e}^{uv}$ ，而 $u = \ln \sqrt{x^2 + y^2},v = \arctan {\frac{y}{x}}$ 求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x},\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}.$   
5. 若 $u = \frac{\mathrm{e}^{ax}(y - z)}{a^2 + 1}$ , 而 $y = a\sin x, z = \cos x$ , 求 $\mathrm{d}u$ .

下列诸题，函数 $F, f, \varphi, \psi$ 都有足够阶的连续导数

6. 若 $u = F(x, y)$ , 而 $x = r \cos \varphi, y = r \sin \varphi$ , 求 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial \varphi}$ .  
7. 若 $z = f(x^{2} - y^{2},\mathrm{e}^{xy})$ ，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x},\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$   
8. 若 $s = f(x^3, xy, xyz)$ , 求 $\frac{\partial s}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial s}{\partial y}, \frac{\partial s}{\partial z}$ .  
9. 若 $z = xyf\left(\frac{x}{y}, \frac{y}{x}\right)$ ，求 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$ .  
10. $u = f(x^{3} + xy + xyz)$ ,求 $\mathrm{d}u$   
11. 设 $z = \arctan \frac{u}{v}$ ，其中 $u = x + y, v = x - y$ ，求证

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {x - y}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}.
$$

12. 设 $z = xy + yf(u)$ , 而 $u = \frac{x}{y}$ , 求证 $x\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = z + xy$ .   
13. 设 $z = \frac{y}{f(x^2 - y^2)}$ ，求证 $\frac{1}{x} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \frac{1}{y} \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac{z}{y^2}$ .  
14. 设 $u = f(x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2})$ ，求 $\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2}, \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2}, \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z^2}$   
15. 设 $u = f(x,xy,xyz)$ , 求 $\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y \partial x}, \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z \partial y}, \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x \partial z}$ .   
16. 设 $y = \varphi(x + \mu t) + \psi(x - \mu t)$ ，求证 $\frac{\partial^2y}{\partial t^2} = \mu^2 \frac{\partial^2y}{\partial x^2}$ .  
17. 设 $z = f(x + \varphi(y))$ ，求证 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \cdot \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x^2}$ .  
18. 令 $u = \ln \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ , $v = \arctan \frac{y}{x}$ , 变换方程 $(x + y)\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} - (x - y)\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0$ .  
19. 证明方程

$$
z _ {x x} ^ {\prime \prime} + 2 x y ^ {2} z _ {x} ^ {\prime} + 2 (y - y ^ {3}) z _ {y} ^ {\prime} + x ^ {2} y ^ {2} z = 0
$$

在变数变换 $x = uv, y = \frac{1}{v}$ 之下形式不变

20. 求隐函数 $z(x, y)$ 的偏导数 $z_x'$ 及 $z_y'$ :

1) $x + 2y + z - 2\sqrt{xyz} = 0;$   
2) $\mathrm{e}^z - xyz = 0$ ;   
3) $\frac{x}{z} = \ln \frac{z}{y}$ .

21. 求由方程 $\cos^2 x + \cos^2 y + \cos^2 z = 1$ 所确定的隐函数 $z = f(x, y)$ 的全微分 $\mathrm{d}z$ .  
22. 设 $x^{3} + y^{3} + z^{3} + xyz = 6$ 确定隐函数 $z = z(x,y)$ ，试求 $\left.\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right|_{(1,2,-1)}$ 及 $\left.\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right|_{(1,2,-1)}$   
23. 设 $2\sin (x + 2y - 3z) = x + 2y - 3z$ ，证明 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 1$ .  
24. 设 $f$ 为可微函数，而隐函数 $z(x, y)$ 由方程 $x + z = yf(x^2 - z^2)$ 定义，证明

$$
z \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} + y \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = x.
$$

25. 设函数 $f$ 有连续偏导数，函数 $z(x, y)$ 由方程 $f\left(x + \frac{z}{y}, y + \frac{z}{x}\right) = 0$ 所确定，证明

$$
x \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} + y \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = z - x y.
$$

29. 设 $x + y + z = 2, x^2 + y^2 = \frac{1}{2} z^2$ , 求 $x = 1, y = -1, z = 2$ 时 $\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{d}z}$ 及 $\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}z}$ 之值.
