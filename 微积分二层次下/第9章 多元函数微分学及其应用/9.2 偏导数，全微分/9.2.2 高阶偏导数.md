如果函数 $z = f(x,y)$ 对 $x$ 及 $y$ 的偏导函数 $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}, \frac{\partial f}{\partial y}$ 对于变量 $x,y$ 又有偏导数，则称之为 $f(x,y)$ 的二阶偏导数，例如 $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$ 对 $x$ 的偏导数 $\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\right)$ 是 $f(x,y)$ 关于 $x$ 的二阶偏导数，记为

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial x ^ {2}},   f _ {x ^ {2}} ^ {\prime \prime},   f _ {x x} ^ {\prime \prime},   \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}},   z _ {x ^ {2}} ^ {\prime \prime} \text {或} z _ {x x} ^ {\prime \prime}.
$$

而 $\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}$ 对 $y$ 的偏导数 $\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}\right)$ 则记为

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial y \partial x}, \quad f _ {x y} ^ {\prime \prime}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y \partial x} \quad \text {或} \quad z _ {x y} ^ {\prime \prime}.
$$

同样， $\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}$ 的两个偏导数 $\frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)$ 和 $\frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{\partial f}{\partial y}\right)$ 依次记为

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial x \partial y}, \quad f _ {y x} ^ {\prime \prime}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x \partial y}, \quad z _ {y x} ^ {\prime \prime}; \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial y ^ {2}}, \quad f _ {y ^ {2}} ^ {\prime \prime}, \quad f _ {y y} ^ {\prime \prime}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}}, \quad z _ {y ^ {2}} ^ {\prime \prime}, \quad z _ {y y} ^ {\prime \prime}. \\ \end{array}
$$

二阶偏导数如果又有偏导数，则称之为三阶偏导数。二阶及二阶以上的偏导数称为高阶偏导数，而 $f_{x}^{\prime}, f_{y}^{\prime}$ 等则称为一阶偏导数。

对于变元多于两个的函数，高阶偏导数可类似地定义．例如，对于三元函数 $u = g(x,y,z)$ 有

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} u}{\partial y \partial z} = u _ {z y} ^ {\prime \prime} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial z}\right),
$$

$$
\frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial z \partial x ^ {2}} = u _ {x ^ {2} z} ^ {\prime \prime \prime} = \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \left(\frac {\partial^ {2} u}{\partial x ^ {2}}\right),
$$

$$
\frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial y ^ {3}} = u _ {y ^ {3}} ^ {\prime \prime \prime} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left(\frac {\partial^ {2} u}{\partial y ^ {2}}\right),
$$

……

由定义可知，求高阶偏导数实际上只不过是逐次求一阶偏导数而已。

例9.2.6 求函数 $z = \mathrm{e}^{xy} + x^2\sin y$ 的所有二阶偏导数

解因为 $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = y\mathrm{e}^{xy} + 2x\sin y,\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = x\mathrm{e}^{xy} + x^2\cos y,$ 所以

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x ^ {2}} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (y \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + 2 x \sin y) = y ^ {2} \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + 2 \sin y, \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y \partial x} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (y \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + 2 x \sin y) = (x y + 1) \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + 2 x \cos y, \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial y ^ {2}} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (x \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + x ^ {2} \cos y) = x ^ {2} \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} - x ^ {2} \sin y, \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (x \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + x ^ {2} \cos y) = (x y + 1) \mathrm {e} ^ {x y} + 2 x \cos y. \\ \end{array}
$$

含有对不同变元求导的高阶偏导数称为混合导数。在例9.2.6， $z(x,y)$ 的两个二阶混合导数 $\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y}$ 和 $\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y \partial x}$ 是相等的。这不是偶然的，我们给出下述定理而略去证明。

**定理9.2.1**（混合导数交换次序）如果函数 $z = f(x,y)$ 的两个二阶混合导数 $\frac{\partial^2f}{\partial x\partial y}$ 和 $\frac{\partial^2f}{\partial y\partial x}$ 在区域 $D$ 内连续，则在该区域内

$$
\frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac {\partial^ {2} f}{\partial y \partial x}.
$$

即：在所述条件下偏导数与求导次序无关

定理9.2.1不但适用于二元函数，也适用变元更多的函数．不但对于二阶混合导数成立，而且还可推广到更高阶的混合导数：只要所涉及的混合导数都是连续的(今后，我们所遇到的正是这种情形)，则结果与求导次序无关.

例9.2.7 设 $u = \mathrm{e}^{xyz}$ ，求 $\frac{\partial^3 u}{\partial z \partial y \partial x}$ .

解

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} = y z \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z}, \\ \frac {\partial^ {2} u}{\partial y \partial x} = \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (y z \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z}) = z \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z} + x y z ^ {2} \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z} \\ = (1 + x y z) z \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial z \partial y \partial x} = \frac {\partial}{\partial z} [ (1 + x y z) z e ^ {x y z} ] \\ = x y \cdot z e ^ {x y z} + (1 + x y z) e ^ {x y z} + (1 + x y z) z \cdot x y e ^ {x y z} \\ = (1 + 3 x y z + x ^ {2} y ^ {2} z ^ {2}) \mathrm {e} ^ {x y z}. \\ \end{array}
$$

其余五个三阶混合导数

$$
\frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial x \partial y \partial z}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial y \partial x \partial z}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial y \partial z \partial x}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial x \partial z \partial y}, \quad \frac {\partial^ {3} u}{\partial z \partial x \partial y}
$$

不必另行计算，结果是和例9.2.7相同的。按变量 $x, y, z$ 的对称性，也可预见这一事实。

在混合导数与求导次序无关的情形，可以交换求导的次序，将对同一个变元的求导手续集中在一起，例如，函数 $f(x,y)$ 对 $x$ 求 $i$ 次导数对 $y$ 求 $m - i$ 次导数可记为 $\frac{\partial^m f}{\partial x^i \partial y^{m - i}}$ 或 $f_{x^i y^{m - i}}^{(m)}$ 。

例9.2.8 设 $z = x^{m}y^{n}$ ( $m, n$ 为正整数), 求 $\frac{\partial^{m + n - 2}f(2,3)}{\partial x^{m - 1}\partial y^{n - 1}}$ 之值.

解因为 $(x^{m})^{(m - 1)} = m!x,(y^{n})^{(n - 1)} = n!y,$ 所以

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial^ {m + n - 2} f}{\partial x ^ {m - 1} \partial y ^ {n - 1}} = \frac {\partial^ {m - 1}}{\partial x ^ {m - 1}} \left(\frac {\partial^ {n - 1} f}{\partial y ^ {n - 1}}\right) = \frac {\partial^ {m - 1}}{\partial x ^ {m - 1}} (x ^ {m} \cdot n! y) \\ = n! y \cdot m! x = n! m! x y. \\ \end{array}
$$

以 $x = 2, y = 3$ 代入得

$$
\frac {\partial^ {m + n - 2} f (2 , 3)}{\partial x ^ {m - 1} \partial y ^ {n - 1}} = 6 \cdot n! m!.
$$
