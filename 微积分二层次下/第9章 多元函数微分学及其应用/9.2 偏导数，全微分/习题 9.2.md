1. 求一阶和二阶偏导数：

1) $u = xy + \frac{y}{x}$ ;   
2) $u = \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}};$   
3) $u = y\sin (x + y)$   
4) $u = \frac{\cos y^2}{x}$ ;   
5) $u = \tan \frac{y^2}{x}$ ;   
6) $u = \arctan \frac{x}{y}$ ;   
7) $u = \ln (x^2 +y)$

2. 求一阶偏导数：

1) $u = \sin (x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2})$   
2) $u = \ln (3x - 2y + z)$ ;   
3) $u = \sin \frac{x}{y}\cos \frac{y}{x} +\arcsin \sqrt{z};$   
4) $u = y^{\frac{z}{x}}$

3. 设 $f(x, y) = x + y - \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ , 求 $f_x'(3, 4)$ .   
4. 设 $z = \frac{x\cos y - y\cos x}{1 + \sin x + \sin y}$ , 求 $\left.\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right|_{\substack{x = 0 \\ y = 0}}$ 及 $\left.\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right|_{\substack{x = 0 \\ y = 0}}$ .   
5. 若 $u = x \ln (xy)$ , 求 $\frac{\partial^3 u}{\partial x^2 \partial y}$ .  
6. 若 $z = \sin^2 (ax + by)$ , 求 $\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y}$ .  
7. 若 $u = x^3 \sin y + y^3 \sin x$ , 求 $\frac{\partial^6 u}{\partial x^3 \partial y^3}$ .

8. 若 $u = \mathrm{e}^{xyz}$ , 求 $\frac{\partial^3 u}{\partial x \partial y \partial z}$ .

9. 若 $f(x, y) = \mathrm{e}^{ax} \sin by$ , 求 $f_{x^m y^n}^{(n + m)}(0, 0)$ .

10. 设 $z = \mathrm{e}^{x}(\cos y + x\sin y)$ , 验证 $\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y \partial x}$ .

11. 设 $u = \frac{1}{2a\sqrt{\pi t}}\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{(x - b)^2}{4a^2t}}$ 试证 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial t} = a^2\frac{\partial^2u}{\partial x^2}$

12. 若 $u = z\arctan \frac{x}{y}$ , 试证 $\frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z^2} = 0$ .

13. 求下列函数的全微分：

1) $u = \frac{x + y}{x - y}$ ;

2) $u = x^{yz}$

14. 求近似值：

1) $\sqrt{(1.02)^3 + (1.97)^3}$ ;

2) $(10.1)^{2.03}$ .

15. 已知矩形的边长 $x = 6$ 米， $y = 8$ 米，若 $x$ 增加 5 厘米， $y$ 减少 10 厘米，问矩形的对角线大约变化多少？
