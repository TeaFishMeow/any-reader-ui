3. 证明曲线 $y = \varphi(x)$ ， $z = \psi(x)$ ，在点 $(x_0, y_0, z_0)$ 的切线与法平面依次为

$$
\frac {x - x _ {0}}{1} = \frac {y - y _ {0}}{\varphi^ {\prime} (x _ {0})} = \frac {z - z _ {0}}{\psi^ {\prime} (x _ {0})}
$$

及

$$
(x - x _ {0}) + (y - y _ {0}) \varphi^ {\prime} (x _ {0}) + (z - z _ {0}) \psi^ {\prime} (x _ {0}) = 0.
$$
